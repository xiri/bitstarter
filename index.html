A lot of businesses face optimization problems constantly. They have certain constraints and want to minimize or maximize some objectives, for examples, a company wants to fit dozens of speakers into minimal hotel rooms for a particular conference during a particular period. For often times, a slightly improved optimization could reduce significant costs or bring more income. On the one hand, some business entities may not have the personnels or resources to model and solve the problems by themselves, even ones that are not overly complex. On the other hand, certain problems are called NP-complete, i.e. as of today, still no one knows how to find the optimal solutions quickly as the size of the problem grows. Generally heuristics from problem insight and/or customized algorithms play a vital role in achieving good approximations.

We strongly believe that, with different constraints, each business, at a certain time, has a unique problem and it would be interesting and effective, generally cheaper, to utilize crowdsourcing. Businesses who want their problems solved can describe their problems and expected optimization degree to us. We will formalize them, defining the problem constraints and objectives, setting up and providing a "competition" platform for those optimization solvers from all over the world to submit their best solutions in a competition-like environment. Winners get financially rewarded while the others should have some fun and opportunity to hone their skills.
